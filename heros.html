Philippine National Heroes

José Rizal

National hero of the Philippines known for his writings that inspired the Philippine revolution against Spanish colonization.

Andrés Bonifacio

Founder of the Katipunan and a leader of the Philippine Revolution.

Emilio Aguinaldo

First President of the Philippines and leader in the Philippine Revolution.

Apolinario Mabini

Known as the "Brains of the Revolution," Mabini was a key adviser to Emilio Aguinaldo.

Gabriela Silang

Filipina revolutionary leader who fought against Spanish colonizers after the death of her husband.

Marcelo H. del Pilar

Journalist, lawyer, and reformist leader advocating for Philippine independence from Spain.
